03 / About

I'm a public-health graduate student building software at the seam between clinical workflow, policy, and emerging AI. My work tends to start with the operational gaps no one wants to own — supply chains, care coordination, accreditation paperwork — and asks what changes when an interface, a model, or an agent gets dropped into them.

Before Berkeley, I worked on JCI accreditation at a cancer hospital in Indonesia. Now I split time between Blue Shield of California, the Institute on Aging, and a few prototypes of my own.
